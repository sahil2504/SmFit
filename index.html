Introduction

A lot of people ask me what keeps you going? I've never really wanted to be a fitness icon or a powerlifter, i do it because i enjoy doing this, for me the best part, the best feeling about this is that when you walk into a gym and the way people look at you with respect irrespective of their age, they want to learn from you, they idealize you. I feel this is what has kept me going all those flattering compliment, respect and support from people.

I know this is not a very big achievement to have a good physique, it's very common these days people with abs and muscles but this is the biggest thing I've achieved on my own. If you can motivate someone to do something after they look at you it's an achievement and it's something you should be proud of and I'm proud of myself!! 4 years back i stepped into the gym for the very first time. Skinny, weak, lack of self-esteem, in doubt and uncertainty about myself, and a feeling of not measuring up to standards but determined to change myself. It's been a long journey with lot's of ups and downs. A journey from a skinny 57 kg guy to a 87 kg confident guy.

Of all these years, this year has been the most irregular, difficult and strenuous one, had to stop lifting for almost 3 months due to bursitis, then supraspinatus surface tear and an infraspinatus tendonitis and now due to this quarantine we all have not been able to hit the gym for months now, But still a very fruitful year in terms of learning and gathering knowledge as i have recently completed my certification in Sports, Exercise and Nutrition!!

There were times when i felt i should leave this and that I'm not good at it, that i'm wasting my time but thank you everyone for being there to help me get up again.Thank you for all the support be it moral or physical, motivation, compliments and love.

Cheers to many more years of love for Fitness!!

CALISTENICS

I started my fitness with calisthenics. "If you can't lift your own bodyweight then what's the use of lifting heavy weights". Calisthenics is not known in india much but every child in one or the other way performs calisthenics during his playtime, may it be trying to jump and grab a bar or by doing simple pushups. I recommend every fitness enthusiast to start with calisthenics first and then jump to weight training.
