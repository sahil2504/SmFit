A lot of people ask me what keeps you going? I've never really wanted to be a fitness icon or a powerlifter, i do it coz i enjoy doing this, for me the best part the best feeling about this is that when you walk into a gym and the way people look at you with respect irrespective of their age, they want to learn from you, they idealize you. I feel this is what has kept me going all those flattering compliment, respect and support from people.

Ik this is not a very big achievement to have a good physique, it's very common these days people with abs and muscles but this is the biggest thing I've achieved on my own. If you can motivate someone to do something after they look at you it's an achievement and it's something you should be proud of and I'm proud of myself!! Exactly 4 years back on this day i stepped into the gym for the very first time. Skinny, weak, lack of self-esteem, in doubt and uncertainty about myself, and a feeling of not measuring up to standards but determined to change myself. It's been a long journey with lot's of ups and downs. A journey from a skinny 57 kg guy to a 87 kg confident guy.

Of all these years, this year has been the most irregular, difficult and strenuous one, had to stop lifting for almost 3 months due to bursitis, then supraspinatus surface tear and an infraspinatus tendonitis. But still a very fruitful year in terms of learning and gaining.

There were times when i felt i should leave this and that I'm not good at it, that i'm wasting my time but thank you everyone for being there to help me get up again.Thank you for all the support be it moral or physical, motivation, compliments and love.

Cheers to many more years of love for Fitness!!
